# Product Positioning

## One-line Positioning

AI-ready Business Website Generator helps small businesses generate websites that are easier for AI assistants to understand, cite, and audit.

## Market Category

AI visibility / GEO / AI-search-ready website starter kit.

## Main Audience

- Small business owners.
- Local service companies.
- Restaurants, hotels, scenic spots, clinics, and agencies.
- SEO / GEO consultants.
- Developers building AI-readable business websites.

## The Problem

Most business websites are not structured for AI answer engines:

- service details are vague,
- customer questions are missing,
- facts are trapped in images or PDFs,
- no `llms.txt`,
- no structured metadata,
- no daily question monitoring.

## The Product Promise

This project helps businesses produce clearer public pages and repeatable AI visibility checks.

It does not guarantee ranking, indexing, citations, or business conversion.

## Positioning Statement

For small businesses that want to become easier for AI assistants to understand, AI-ready Business Website Generator is a public-safe website starter kit that turns company facts, services, and customer questions into static pages, metadata, and AI visibility checklists.
